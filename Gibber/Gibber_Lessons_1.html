/*Welcome to Gibber!

The first thing we need to know is how to 

execute our code. Move your cursor to 

line six and press "control & enter" */


a =Sine(440)


/*Great! Now press "control & period"

This stops gibber entirely, as you can see 

in the bottom of the browser "Gibber has

been cleared."


There are alternatives to clearing Gibber

if you want your sounds to stop. Re-execute

line six, then execute line 17, 18 and 19*/


a.disconnect()

a.connect()

a._


/*this way gibber can still run other sounds

and let you connect or disconnect new sounds,

without interupting the music. execute line 

25 and then try executing and disconnecting 

the Sine(440) */


b=Drums('xo**-...')


/* You can always press "control & period" to

stop gibber. 


to execute more than one line of gibber press

"alt & enter" on line 35. */


c=Synth()

c.note.seq([0,2,4,5,6,8,10],1/8)

/*you will hear a series of notes, plus an added

effect. Execute line 42 to gradually fade out */


c.amp = Seq([,.9,.8,.7,.6,.5,.4,.3,.2,.1,.0],1/8)


/* Note that c.amp is not a dissconnection, the 

synth is still running. Execute line 48 to set the

amplitude back up. */
